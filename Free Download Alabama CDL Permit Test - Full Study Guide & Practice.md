## Steps to Getting Your Alabama CDL

Here's a brief overview of the steps involved in getting your Alabama CDL:

1.  **Meet the Eligibility Requirements:** Be at least 18 years old (21 for interstate driving), hold a valid Alabama driver's license, and meet the medical requirements.
2.  **Study the Alabama CDL Manual:** Familiarize yourself with the regulations and requirements outlined in the official manual.
3.  **Pass the Alabama CDL Permit Test:** Pass the required knowledge tests to obtain your commercial learner's permit (CLP). This Udemy course will help you prepare.
4.  **Hold Your CLP for at Least 14 Days:** You must hold your CLP for a minimum of 14 days before you can take the skills test.
5.  **Pass the Alabama CDL Skills Test:** Pass the skills test, which includes a pre-trip inspection, basic control skills, and a road test.
6.  **Obtain Your CDL:** Once you pass the skills test, you will receive your Alabama CDL.
